THE MODULE IN ACTION

See Field Sales Inside Odoo 18

Real screenshots of the Field Sales kiosk portal, client visit logging, route trajectory map, and manager auditing workflow.

1. Mobile Kiosk Portal — Start Shift / End Shift / Log Visit KIOSK LANDING
Touch-optimized mobile kiosk portal accessible from smartphone, tablet, or desktop browser. Sales agents can start their shift, record client meetings, or check out with a single click.
2. Selfie Camera Verification & High-Accuracy GPS Check-In GPS & BIOMETRIC PROOF
Clicking "Check In" launches an integrated camera modal that captures an agent selfie and locks real-time HTML5 GPS latitude, longitude, and accuracy radius before opening shift.
3. Active Session Portal & Live Shift Status ACTIVE SESSION
Once checked in, the portal displays active session parameters including shift start timestamp, agent name, and a prominent "Log Client Visit" button for on-the-field activity.
4. Streamlined Client Visit Logger LOG VISIT FORM
Intuitive modal dialog designed for fast entry in the field. Reps fill in customer business name, contact person, phone number, and meeting outcome notes.
5. Mandatory Storefront / Client Meeting Photo Verification STOREFRONT PROOF
To prevent remote or fake logging, reps must capture a photo of the client storefront, business card, or meeting space. The image is stored as binary data in Odoo.
6. Structured Client Visit Logging & Geo-Tagging GEO-TAGGED VISIT
Every saved visit automatically locks current GPS coordinates, links Google Maps navigation, and creates/updates Res.Partner contact records tagged as 'Field Lead'.
7. Session Client Visit Feed & History Audit VISIT HISTORY
View a list of all visits recorded during the current shift, complete with client company name, contact details, timestamp, photo preview, and GPS coordinates.
8. Real-Time Work Session KPI Dashboard LIVE SESSION KPIS
Live productivity feed updating total visit counts, active duration, and representative status in real-time on the kiosk interface.
9. Shift Check-Out Initiation Modal CHECKOUT CONFIRM
When finishing work, clicking "Check Out" opens a final shift wrap-up modal to confirm shift end and initialize check-out photo verification.
10. Final Check-Out Selfie & Distance Audit FINAL CHECKOUT PROOF
Captures second selfie verification photo and end-of-day GPS coordinates. The Haversine distance engine calculates distance deviation from check-in point.
11. Backend Manager Work Session Audit Record ODOO BACKEND FORM
Complete Odoo backend form view showing shift duration, verification photos, geolocation logs, distance deviation metrics, and direct Google Maps hyper-links.
12. Interactive Leaflet Route Trajectory Map LEAFLET ROUTE MAP
Interactive map view tracing sales representative travel path throughout the day with distinct visual markers for Check-In (Green), Client Visits (Blue), and Check-Out (Red).
13. Automated End-of-Day PDF Session Report QWEB PDF EXPORT
Export presentation-ready PDF reports with shift summary tables, selfie proofs, client visit notes, storefront photos, and manager audit signatures.
14. Central Manager Overview & Field Analytics MANAGER DASHBOARD
High-level management view allowing supervisors to monitor field reps across different regions, track compliance metrics, and review total logged visits.
